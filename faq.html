Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

1. What services does Sauce provide?

We offer video editing services ranging from corporate videos, social media content, to cinematic editing. See our Services page for more details.

2. How do I get started with Sauce?

You can start by contacting us through the Contact Us page or selecting a pricing plan. We'll guide you through the process and help you upload your footage.

3. What file formats do you accept?

We accept MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, and other common video file formats. If you’re unsure, reach out to us, and we’ll confirm if we can work with your file format.

Process & Turnaround

4. How long does it take to complete a video edit?

The turnaround time varies by project. Basic edits take 3-5 days, while more complex edits may take up to 7-10 days. Expedited services are available upon request.

5. Can I request revisions?

Yes, revisions are included with each plan. The number of revisions depends on the plan you choose. Additional revisions may incur a small fee.

6. How do I send my video files?

We accept file uploads through platforms like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. We’ll provide instructions once you confirm your project.

Pricing & Payment

7. What are your pricing options?

We have flexible pricing plans based on the type of editing, video length, and turnaround time. Visit our Pricing page for detailed information.

8. Do you offer discounts for bulk projects?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk or long-term projects. Contact us for a custom quote.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. A deposit is required before starting your project.

Technical & Creative

10. Can you add music or sound effects to my video?

Yes, we can add music, sound effects, and voiceovers. You can provide your own or request royalty-free tracks.

11. Do you offer color correction and special effects?

We provide color correction and special effects, especially in our advanced and premium packages. Check out our Pricing page for more details.

12. Can you edit videos for YouTube, Instagram, or other platforms?

Yes, we edit videos tailored for specific platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. We ensure the video format and length meet the platform’s requirements.

Other Questions

13. What happens if I'm not satisfied with the final video?

We aim for your satisfaction. You can request revisions, and we will work closely with you to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

14. Can I cancel my project?

Yes, you can cancel, but you will be charged for the work completed so far. We recommend discussing the cancellation process with us directly.

15. Do you offer long-term video editing services?

We provide long-term services for content creators, businesses, and agencies. Contact us for ongoing video editing solutions.
